Wedding Package

Mr & Mrs Hill

A total package including a custom mark for their big day, Save the Date postcards, formal invitations, RSVPs and inserts. Beyond standard print materials, the couple requested artwork to print on wine and pint glasses that could be used, and serve as a keepsake for guests. Additionally, their full website accepted donations, hosted their registry, allowed guests to rsvp, and contained key information for attendees.
